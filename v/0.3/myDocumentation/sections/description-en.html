The classes related to the information content entities are shown in the figure below. The root concept is inherited from BFO and IAO, as redefined in Entities ontology as dice:InformationContentEntity. Some information entities are defined in Variables ontology (dicv:Variable, dicv:Constraint, dicv:VariableDomain)and some in the Entities ontology (dice:Labeling, dice:Identifier, dice:Category). Rest of the information entities are defined in the Information ontology. The hierarchy shown in the figure is only partial, and do not show all detailed classes.
