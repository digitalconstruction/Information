Abstract

Digital Construction Information ontology defines the concepts and relations for information content entities in construction and renovation domain. Examples of such entities are BIM models, drawings, point clouds, plans, contracts, issues, and messages. It also includes more technical information enties such as variables, labels, scenarios, and so on. The model covers specifically the different information contents and does not address the question on which physical physical carriers (paper, disk, claoud storage, ...) the content is stored.
